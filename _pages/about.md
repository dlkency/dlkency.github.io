I am a PhD candidate in the Civil & Environmental Engineering, [Center for Digital Asset Finance](https://www.difin.io/) at the University of Michigan. I am interested in building models and testing the impact of climate changes, ESG factors and green bond issues on bond pricing. Tools I use heavily include causal inference, machine learning and various statistical models.
